Five data files to be used on Kimmo's lecture on the IDS2024 course (7.10.2024)

File: happiness.csv
Happiness and other scores from various countries
Source: kaggle.com/datasets

File: global.csv
Measures of global development from various countries
Source: kaggle.com/datasets

File: deca.csv
The 48 best athletes of decathlon in 1973: name, scores, height, and weight
Source: survo.fi/books

File: fossils.csv
Teeth measurements (as discriminant scores) of various ape species and fossils
Source: survo.fi/books

File: Firstbeat.csv
Firstbeat measurements of one individual
Source: one study in WELLS research group
